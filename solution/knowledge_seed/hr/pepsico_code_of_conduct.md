# Pepsico Code of Conduct (Excerpt)

## Our Commitments
We act with integrity. We follow the law. We respect each other and our
consumers. We protect Pepsico information and assets.

## Conflicts of Interest
Disclose any personal relationship, outside employment, financial interest,
or gift that could influence your work decisions. Disclosures are made
through the **EthicsPoint** portal and are reviewed by the Ethics & Compliance
team within 10 business days.

## Gifts and Hospitality
- Gifts under **$100 (USD)** in fair market value may generally be accepted
  from a non-government party without prior approval.
- Gifts of any value to or from government officials must be pre-approved.
- Cash and cash equivalents (gift cards) are never permitted.

## Anti-Harassment
Pepsico has zero tolerance for harassment or discrimination. Report concerns
to your manager, HR business partner, or anonymously through the **Speak Up**
hotline (1-866-PEP-SAFE). Retaliation is strictly prohibited.

## Data Privacy
Handle consumer and associate data only for legitimate business purposes and
in line with regional privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA, LGPD). Report suspected
data incidents to the Privacy Office within 24 hours.
