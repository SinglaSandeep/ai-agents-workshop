# 2025 SuperBowl Post-Mortem

## What worked

- Hero spot delivered +9% aided brand recall in 13–22 (target was +8%).
- TikTok creator activation drove 38M views, 22% above plan.
- Co-branded NCAA segment lifted same-store sales 3.6% in partner regions.

## What did not

- Programmatic OTT spend underperformed (CPM 2.1x of in-arena LED). Cut by
  60% in 2026 plan.
- Spanish-language cutdown shipped 6 days late, missing the pre-game window.

## Recommendations for 2026

1. Lock the multilingual cutdown delivery 14 days before air.
2. Reallocate OTT budget into TikTok creator and in-arena LED.
3. Bring measurement partner on at brief stage, not at QA stage.
