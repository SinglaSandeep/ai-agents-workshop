# Redmond Store — Manager Handbook

**Address.** 17725 NE 65th St, Redmond, WA.
**Hours.** Mon–Sat 6 AM – 9 PM, Sun 7 AM – 8 PM.

## Local context

Redmond serves a tech-corridor demographic with significant suburban-home
remodel demand. Smart-home electrical and high-end power-tool sales are
both above chain average. The Pro Desk handles a steady stream of
solar-install electricians.

## Local discount-approval guidance

Per the chain matrix the store manager approves up to **20%**. Smart-home
bundle promotions are pre-approved campaign discounts (see active
campaigns) and do not require manager sign-off.

## Inventory specialties

- BrightHome smart lighting + switches.
- TorqueMax cordless premium tools.
- Composite-decking samples and contractor consults.

## Recurring local events

- **Smart-home Sunday** — second Sunday of each month, 1 PM demo.
- **Deck-build workshop** — Saturdays in April and May.
