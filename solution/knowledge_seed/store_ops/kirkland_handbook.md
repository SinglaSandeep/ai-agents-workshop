# Kirkland Store — Manager Handbook

**Address.** 12525 116th Ave NE, Kirkland, WA.
**Hours.** Mon–Sat 7 AM – 9 PM, Sun 8 AM – 7 PM.

## Local context

Kirkland is a smaller-footprint Eastside store focused on homeowner-DIY.
No lumber yard — lumber and sheet goods are special-order via Bellevue or
Redmond and delivered direct to customer or held for in-store pickup.

## Local discount-approval guidance

Per the chain matrix the store manager approves up to **20%**. Special-
order delivery fees are not subject to the matrix and are quoted at cost.

## Inventory specialties

- Paint, garden, hand tools, hardware (full assortment).
- Limited power-tool floor — special-order high-ticket SKUs from Bellevue.

## Recurring local events

- **Saturday DIY workshops** — every Saturday 10 AM.
- **Kid-craft Thursdays** — first Thursday of each month, 4 PM in the
  paint aisle.
