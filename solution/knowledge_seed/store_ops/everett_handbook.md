# Everett Store — Manager Handbook

**Address.** 1305 SE Everett Mall Way, Everett, WA.
**Hours.** Mon–Sat 6 AM – 9 PM, Sun 7 AM – 8 PM.

## Local context

Everett serves Snohomish County north of the Seattle metro. The customer
mix includes Boeing-area homeowners and a steady volume of independent
contractors. Lumber sells well; the yard hosts a partial assortment with
overflow special-ordered from Tacoma.

## Local discount-approval guidance

Per the chain matrix the store manager approves up to **20%**. Composite-
decking custom-cut orders go through the Pro Desk quote workflow.

## Inventory specialties

- Pressure-treated lumber, fencing, sheathing.
- Outdoor power-tool special-order (mowers, blowers).

## Recurring local events

- **Boeing-shift Saturday breakfast** — second Saturday each month, 5 AM.
- **Spring fencing clinic** — early April.
