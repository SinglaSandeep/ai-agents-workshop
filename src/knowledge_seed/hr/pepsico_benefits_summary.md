# Pepsico Benefits Summary

## Health Plans
Pepsico offers three medical plan options through the **MyHealth** portal:
- **PPO Standard** — broad network, modest premiums, $1,000 individual deductible.
- **PPO Premium** — lowest deductible ($500), highest premiums, best coverage for
  associates with ongoing care needs.
- **HDHP + HSA** — high-deductible health plan paired with a Health Savings
  Account. Pepsico contributes $750 per individual / $1,500 per family annually.

All plans include preventive care at 100% in-network with no deductible.

## Retirement
- **401(k) Savings Plan** — eligible after 30 days of service.
- **Company match**: dollar-for-dollar on the first 5% of eligible pay.
- Immediate vesting on associate contributions; company match vests after
  3 years of service.

## Wellness
- $300 annual wellness reimbursement (gym, fitness apps, mental wellbeing apps).
- 8 free counseling sessions per year through the Employee Assistance Program (EAP).

## Parental Leave
- 12 weeks of fully-paid parental leave for the primary caregiver.
- 4 weeks of fully-paid leave for the non-primary caregiver.
- Available for birth, adoption, or foster placement.

## Tuition
Up to **$10,000 per calendar year** for approved degree or certificate programs
in fields aligned with Pepsico's business. Requires manager and Talent
Development approval before enrollment.
