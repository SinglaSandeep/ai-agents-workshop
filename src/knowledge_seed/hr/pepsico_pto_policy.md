# Pepsico Paid Time Off (PTO) Policy

## Overview
Pepsico provides paid time off (PTO) to allow associates to rest, attend to
personal matters, and care for their families. PTO is a single bucket that
covers vacation, sick, and personal days for most U.S. salaried associates.

## Annual Accruals (U.S. salaried)
| Tenure                   | Annual PTO  |
|--------------------------|-------------|
| 0 - 1 year (pro-rated)   | 15 days     |
| 1 - 5 years              | 20 days     |
| 5 - 10 years             | 25 days     |
| 10+ years                | 30 days     |

PTO accrues at 1/24th of the annual amount each pay period.

## Requesting PTO
- Submit requests through Pepsico MyTime at least **2 weeks in advance** for
  blocks of 3+ consecutive days.
- Manager approval is required; managers should respond within 5 business days.
- For unplanned sick PTO, notify your manager by your scheduled start time.

## Carryover and Payout
- Up to **5 unused PTO days** may carry over to the following calendar year.
- Unused PTO above the cap is forfeited on December 31.
- Upon separation, accrued and unused PTO is paid out where required by law.

## Holiday Schedule
Pepsico observes 10 paid U.S. holidays each year, listed in the annual
holiday calendar published every November on the MyHR portal.
