# Pepsico Remote and Hybrid Work Guidelines

## Eligibility
Hybrid work is available to most corporate roles. Field, manufacturing, and
distribution roles that require physical presence are excluded.

## Standard Schedule
- **Minimum 3 days per week** in the assigned Pepsico office.
- Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday are the default anchor days for most teams.
- Managers may approve alternate schedules where business needs allow.

## Equipment
- Pepsico provides a laptop, monitor, and approved peripherals for the office
  workstation.
- A one-time **$500 home-office stipend** is available for ergonomic equipment
  (chair, desk, lighting). Receipts must be submitted within 90 days of hire
  or schedule change.

## Security Requirements
- Always connect through the Pepsico VPN when accessing internal systems.
- No company data may be stored on personal devices.
- Multi-factor authentication is required for all corporate accounts.

## International Remote Work
Working outside your assigned country requires advance approval from Mobility
and HR Compliance. Pre-approved short-term arrangements (up to 30 days/year)
are documented in the Global Mobility Handbook.
