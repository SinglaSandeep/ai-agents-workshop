# Pro Power-Tool Days 2025 — Post-Mortem

## Headline result
- Power-tool revenue: **+22% YoY** vs. +15% target — a strong overperform.
- ROAS: **2.18×** (target 2.0×).
- New Zava Pro signups: 740 vs. 700 target.

## What worked
- Pro Breakfast events were the single biggest source of incremental
  signups — every store with an event hit its signup target by Day 10.
- YouTube creative on the impact-driver (`ZV-PWT-003`) had a 4.1% CTR,
  best of any power-tool ad we've ever run.
- Free-battery attach drove a 31% lift in cordless-tool basket size.

## What didn't work
- Bellevue ran out of `ZV-PWT-001` kits twice mid-campaign. Inventory was
  in-house chain-wide but FC restock to Bellevue lagged because of the
  same overnight-window contention as the paint category.
- Margin compression from the free-battery promo was steeper than
  modeled. Recommendation: cap the offer to the first 200 kits per store
  in 2026.

## Recommendations carried into 2026
1. Keep Pro Breakfast events on Day 1 — they're the conversion engine.
2. Add Kirkland to the 2026 campaign (was excluded in 2025).
3. Pre-position TorqueMax inventory at the FC and at Bellevue.
4. Cap free-battery offer at first 200 kits per store.
