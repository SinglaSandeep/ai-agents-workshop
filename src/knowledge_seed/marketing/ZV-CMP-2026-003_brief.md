# Garden Kickoff Weekend 2026 — Campaign Brief

## Goal
Capture early-season garden share against regional competitors during the
first official weekend of spring.

## Offer
- 25% off Greenline-branded garden tools, hoses, soil.
- BOGO 50% on potting mix (`ZV-GDN-003`).
- Free seedling with any $25+ garden purchase.

## Stores
All brick-and-mortar PNW stores (no online — physical traffic event).

## Channels
- In-store entrance signage, garden-aisle takeover.
- Radio spots on three regional stations (Seattle, Tacoma, Spokane).
- Social media: Instagram + Facebook.

## Key dates
- Launch: March 21 (first day of spring).
- Wrap: March 29.

## KPIs
- Foot traffic: +25% vs. previous weekend.
- Garden-category revenue: +30% WoW.
- Free-seedling redemption: 60% of qualifying transactions.

## Risks
- PNW weather is unpredictable in March. Contingency: secondary
  "weather-make-up" weekend April 4–5 if Saturday March 21 has heavy rain
  forecast for any store.
- Seedling inventory must arrive at all stores by March 19.
