# Deck-Building Workshop Series 2026 — Campaign Brief

## Goal
Drive an attach-anchored sales motion: every workshop attendee leaves
with a materials-quote and a 10% off-coupon for their first project
purchase.

## Offer
- Free 90-minute Saturday workshop, April–June.
- 10% off composite decking and deck hardware for the next 30 days
  after workshop attendance.
- Featured SKUs: `ZV-LMB-002`, `ZV-LMB-006`, `ZV-HDW-002`.

## Stores
Bellevue, Redmond, Kirkland (Eastside DIY market).

## Channels
- Email to Zava loyalty + Pro lists.
- Workshop registration on zava.com.
- Eventbrite cross-promotion.

## KPIs
- Workshop seats filled: 90% of capacity.
- Workshop → first-purchase conversion: 35%.
- Avg basket on coupon redemption: $1,200.

## Risks
- Kirkland has no on-site lumber yard — quote workflow must route through
  Bellevue or Redmond.
- Composite-decking margin is variable based on supplier rebate.
