# Pro Power-Tool Days 2026 — Campaign Brief

## Goal
Acquire 800 new Zava Pro members and drive 15% YoY growth in power-tool
revenue at participating stores during the March 15 – April 15 window.

## Offer
- 15% off TorqueMax cordless tool kits and bare tools.
- Free 2nd battery with any TorqueMax 20V kit (`ZV-PWT-001`, `ZV-PWT-003`).
- $10 gift card with new Zava Pro signups during the window.

## Stores
Seattle, Bellevue, Redmond, Kirkland, online.

## Channels
- In-store endcap, demo bay, Pro Desk.
- Pro newsletter + targeted email.
- YouTube pre-roll on tool / DIY content; Instagram for the consumer
  segment.

## Key dates
- Launch: March 15 (Pro Breakfast events at all four PNW stores).
- Wrap: April 15.

## KPIs
- Power-tool revenue: +15% YoY.
- New Zava Pro signups: 800.
- ROAS target: 2.0× (driven heavily by Pro lifetime-value modeling).

## Risks
- Inventory of the impact driver (`ZV-PWT-003`) is tight at Spokane —
  though Spokane is not a participating store, demand spillover from
  Eastern WA could leave us under-stocked for the chain.
- Battery free-with-purchase model is a margin hit; offset depends on
  attach rate of accessories (bits, blades) at the same trip.
