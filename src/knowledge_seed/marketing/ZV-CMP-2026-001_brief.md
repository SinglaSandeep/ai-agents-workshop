# Spring Paint Sale 2026 — Campaign Brief

## Insight → Action (read first)
**Business problem:** Spring is Zava's biggest paint window, but last year
(2025) the Seattle flagship store stocked out of the doorbuster SKU
`ZV-PNT-001` twice and missed the YoY target. We are running the same promo
mechanic again in 2026 — so the risk is repeatable and avoidable.

| Insight (what the data shows) | Action (what to do) |
|-------------------------------|---------------------|
| Seattle paint demand spikes on the first two promo weekends and runs ahead of replenishment. | Pre-position 200+ units of `ZV-PNT-001` at Seattle **before** launch. |
| 2025 lost ~60 units / ~$1,800 to overnight-restock lag. | Auto-trigger FC restock when Seattle paint cover drops below 30 units. |
| The 2-for-$60 bundle drives a high attach rate that the base forecast misses. | Raise the Seattle paint reorder threshold from 15 to 25 for Apr–May. |

This is the **killer demo**: the orchestrator joins live Sales (soft trend),
Inventory (low Seattle paint cover) and this Marketing brief + the 2025
post-mortem to recommend a concrete restock + threshold action.

## Goal
Drive 20% YoY growth in paint-category sales during the April–May
window, with a focus on interior renovation projects and the Premium
Interior Paint line.

## Offer
- 20% off all Zava Pro and Zava Basics interior + exterior paint.
- Featured SKUs: `ZV-PNT-001`, `ZV-PNT-002`, `ZV-PNT-005`.
- 2-for-$60 bundle on Premium Interior Eggshell (`ZV-PNT-001`).

## Stores
Seattle, Bellevue, Tacoma, online. (No Spokane / Eastern WA — separate
fall campaign targets that market.)

## Channels
- In-store endcap + tinter-counter signage.
- Email blast to the Zava loyalty list (two sends: 4/1 launch, 4/22 mid).
- Paid social on Instagram and Pinterest, geo-targeted to Seattle metro.

## Key dates
- Launch: April 1.
- Mid-campaign refresh: April 22 (Earth Day tie-in).
- Wrap: May 31.

## Inventory plan
- 1.4× normal paint receipts for April / May.
- Seattle store: pre-position 200 units of `ZV-PNT-001` based on 2025
  post-mortem learnings.
- Online safety stock: 250 units of each featured SKU.

## KPIs
- Paint-category revenue: +20% YoY.
- Mixer-counter throughput: 150 gallons/day at Seattle on peak weekends.
- Email open rate: >24%, click-through >4%.
- ROAS target: 1.5×.

## Risks
1. **Seattle paint inventory under-position.** The 2025 post-mortem
   flagged stock-outs on `ZV-PNT-001` at the Seattle store during the
   first two weekends. Mitigation: 1.4× receipts and overnight restock
   from the online FC.
2. Late spring weather in Seattle suppresses exterior-paint demand.
   Contingency: shift social budget to interior-painting creative.
3. Tinter-counter throughput at Seattle during peak weekends.
   Contingency: schedule two associates at the mixer on Sat / Sun.
