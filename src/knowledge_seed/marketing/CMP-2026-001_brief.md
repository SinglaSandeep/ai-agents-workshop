# CMP-2026-001 — Gatorade SuperBowl Brief

**Campaign code:** CMP-2026-001
**Brand:** Gatorade
**Quarter:** Q1 2026
**Target audience:** Youth athletes (ages 13–22) in the US
**Channels:** TV (SuperBowl spot), Instagram Reels, TikTok, in-arena LED.

## Goal

Reposition Gatorade as the *default hydration choice* for high-school and
college athletes by emphasising the brand's electrolyte science, contrasted
with sugar-water competitors.

## KPIs

- +12% aided brand awareness in the 13–22 segment by end of Q1.
- 30M+ social impressions across IG Reels + TikTok in the seven days
  surrounding the SuperBowl.
- 4% lift in same-store sales of Gatorade Zero in the two weeks following.

## Budget

USD 18M total. 60% TV, 30% social, 10% in-arena.

## Creative direction

Hero spot stars three multi-sport youth athletes. Tagline: *"Win the second
half."* Co-branded with NCAA partner schools where available.
