# Tacoma Store — Manager Handbook

**Address.** 4502 S 56th St, Tacoma, WA.
**Hours.** Mon–Sat 6 AM – 9 PM, Sun 7 AM – 8 PM.

## Local context

Tacoma's customer mix skews toward independent contractors, fishing-fleet
crews from the Tideflats, and South Sound homeowners. The store has the
**largest lumber yard** in the chain after the online fulfillment center
and frequently runs special-order cedar and pressure-treated lumber for
marine and dock construction.

## Local discount-approval guidance

Per the chain matrix the store manager approves up to **20%**. Bulk lumber
orders over $5,000 go through the Pro Desk quote workflow regardless of
discount percentage.

## Inventory specialties

- Marine-grade hardware, pressure-treated lumber.
- Independent-plumber loyalty program (FlowRight brand promotions).

## Recurring local events

- **Saw-and-cut Saturdays** — extended yard hours every Saturday morning.
- **Fishing-fleet hardware run** — first Monday of each month.
