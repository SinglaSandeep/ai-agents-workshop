# Bellevue Store — Manager Handbook

**Address.** 14600 NE 20th St, Bellevue, WA.
**Hours.** Mon–Sat 6 AM – 9 PM, Sun 7 AM – 8 PM.

## Local context

Bellevue serves the Eastside professional and contractor population.
**Pro Desk** is the largest revenue stream — staff it with a dedicated Pro
account manager during weekday business hours. Sustained traffic peaks
3 PM–6 PM weekdays from professional pickup orders.

## Local discount-approval guidance

The Bellevue store manager is authorized to approve discounts up to
**20%** per the chain matrix. Bellevue has a high incidence of price-match
requests against eastside competitors; remember that price match is
**not** a discount under the matrix.

## Inventory specialties

- Smart-home electrical (BrightHome line) — Bellevue is a designated hub.
- Composite decking demos — sample boards in the lumber yard year-round.

## Recurring local events

- **Pro Day** — second Wednesday each month, 6:00 AM – 8:00 AM.
- **Deck-build workshop** — Saturdays in April and May.
