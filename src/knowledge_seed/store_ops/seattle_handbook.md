# Seattle Store — Manager Handbook

**Address.** 1801 Aurora Ave N, Seattle, WA — flagship Pacific Northwest store.
**Hours.** Mon–Sat 6 AM – 9 PM, Sun 7 AM – 8 PM.
**Manager-on-duty radio channel.** 1.

## Local context

Seattle is Zava's flagship store and the highest-traffic location in the
chain. It is also the **regional paint hub** — the in-store tinter handles
overflow demand from Bellevue and Tacoma during Spring Paint Sale season.
Plan for paint-aisle congestion during any paint-category campaign.

## Local discount-approval guidance

Refer to the chain-wide **Discount Approval Matrix** for all bands. As the
flagship store, Seattle's store manager is authorized to approve up to
**20%** without district sign-off. Anything above 20% — including
manager-goodwill refunds on tinted paint defects — must go to the district
manager (district@zava).

## Paint counter staffing

During paint campaigns (Spring Paint Sale, Fall Color Refresh) staff the
mixer station with **two associates** Friday afternoon through Sunday
evening. Cross-train at least four associates per quarter on the tinter.

## Recurring local events

- **Saturday DIY workshops** — every Saturday 10 AM in the demo bay.
- **Pro Breakfast** — first Tuesday each month, 6:30 AM at the Pro Desk.
- **Seafair float build** — third weekend in July, lumber yard reserved.
