# New-Hire Onboarding

The Zava 14-day onboarding plan applies to every full-time and part-time
associate hired at any Pacific Northwest store or the online fulfillment
center.

## Week 1 — Foundations
1. **Day 1.** Paperwork (I-9, W-4, direct deposit, NDA), badge issue,
   uniform fitting, store tour, meet-the-team.
2. **Day 2.** Safety Handbook reading + signed acknowledgement.
   Hands-on: PPE checkout, fire-extinguisher and AED locations,
   evacuation route.
3. **Day 3.** POS training (Module 1: rings, returns, lookups).
4. **Day 4.** Categories deep-dive #1 — paint and stains. Includes the
   mixer station and the tinted-paint return rule.
5. **Day 5.** Shadow a senior associate on the floor.

## Week 2 — Specialization
6. **Day 6–7.** Department assignment + category deep-dive #2.
7. **Day 8.** Pro Desk and contractor workflow.
8. **Day 9.** Customer-incident scenarios + de-escalation training.
9. **Day 10.** First independent shift, with shift lead available.

## Benefits & enrollment
New associates have **30 days** from start date to enroll in medical,
dental, vision, and 401(k) plans. Late enrollment requires a qualifying
life event. Enroll through the People Hub portal.

## Probation period
The first 90 days are a probationary period. The store manager schedules
30/60/90-day check-ins. Performance issues during this window may end in
release; everywhere else, follow the progressive-discipline policy.

## Tools / system access
- POS account: created on Day 1 by store manager.
- Workforce scheduler login: provided on Day 1.
- Pro Desk quoting tool: granted at end of Week 2 if assigned.

All onboarding completion is tracked in the People Hub onboarding
dashboard; the store manager closes out the record at end of Week 2.
