# Spokane Store — Manager Handbook

**Address.** 9420 N Newport Hwy, Spokane, WA.
**Hours.** Mon–Sat 6 AM – 9 PM, Sun 7 AM – 8 PM.

## Local context

Spokane is Zava's only Eastern Washington store. The market is more rural
than the I-5 corridor stores; ranch and agricultural-related hardware, dog-
fencing, and propane exchange sell strongly. Winter-storm prep season
(November–February) is a major revenue driver — keep generator and
salt/ice-melt assortments fully stocked.

## Local discount-approval guidance

Per the chain matrix the store manager approves up to **20%**. Bulk feed-
store competitor price matches are common; document the proof in the POS
transaction.

## Inventory specialties

- Propane exchange (outside the lumber gate).
- Snow-removal equipment, ice melt, generators.
- Agricultural hardware (fencing, livestock waterers).

## Recurring local events

- **Storm-prep clinic** — first Saturday of November.
- **Ag-day open house** — late March.
