# Returns & Exchanges Policy

Zava accepts returns of most items within **90 days** of purchase with a
receipt or member-lookup, in new and resaleable condition.

## Standard return windows

| Category               | Window  | Notes                                            |
|------------------------|---------|--------------------------------------------------|
| Paint (unopened)       | 90 days | Tinted paint is non-returnable unless defective. |
| Power tools            | 90 days | Original packaging preferred; battery sealed.    |
| Hand tools             | 90 days | Lifetime warranty handled separately.            |
| Lumber & building mat. | 30 days | Must be uncut; receipts required.                |
| Special / cut orders   | None    | No returns; document at quote time.              |
| Plants & live goods    | 1 year  | Greenline 1-year live-plant guarantee.           |

## Without a receipt
Lookup by Zava Pro / membership account always counts as a receipt.
Otherwise, returns without a receipt may be issued as store credit at the
lowest 90-day price, subject to store-manager approval.

## Defective items
Defective items can be returned any time within the manufacturer's warranty
period. Out-of-window defectives go through the warranty desk, not standard
returns.

## Tinted paint exception
**Tinted paint cannot be returned for refund**, even within 90 days,
because it cannot be resold. Associates should explain this at the mixing
counter and offer in-store re-tinting at cost if the color is wrong. If the
paint itself is defective (separation, contamination, dried in can), escalate
to the store manager for a goodwill refund — see the discount-approval
matrix for the appropriate band.

## Online orders returned in-store
All Pacific Northwest stores accept online returns. Use the Online Returns
queue in POS; the system will pull shipping cost rules automatically.
