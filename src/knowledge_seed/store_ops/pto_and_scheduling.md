# PTO & Scheduling Policy

## Accruals
- **Full-time** (32+ hours/week): 18 PTO days/year (1.5 days/month accrual).
- **Part-time** (under 32 hours/week): 9 PTO days/year (0.75 days/month).
- Sick leave is separate: 1 hour per 30 hours worked, per Washington State.

PTO begins accruing on Day 1. New hires may use PTO after their 90-day
probation period closes.

## Carryover
Up to 5 PTO days carry over into the next calendar year. Anything above
that is paid out in the January 15 paycheck at base hourly rate.

## Requesting PTO
- File requests in the Workforce scheduler at least **14 days** in advance
  for any block of 3+ consecutive days.
- Single-day requests: at least 48 hours in advance when possible.
- Approval is granted in order received, subject to store coverage. The
  store manager is the approving authority for all PTO at their store.

## Blackout windows
Each store has up to two blackout windows per year where PTO requests
require district-manager approval. These are published in November for
the following year — common windows are the week before Black Friday,
the week between Christmas and New Year, and the Spring Paint Sale
peak weekend.

## Sick leave
No advance notice required, but associates must notify the shift lead
**at least 2 hours** before the scheduled shift when possible. Sick leave
of 3+ consecutive days requires a doctor's note before returning.

## Shift swaps
Associates may swap shifts directly using the scheduler app, subject to
shift-lead approval. Swaps that move someone into overtime require store
manager approval.
