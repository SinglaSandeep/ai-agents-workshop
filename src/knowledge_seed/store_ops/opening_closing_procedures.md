# Opening & Closing Procedures

These procedures apply to every brick-and-mortar Zava store. The online
fulfillment center follows a separate 24/7 operations playbook.

## Opening (60 minutes before doors open)
1. Manager arrives, disarms alarm, completes the morning walk-through.
2. Verify lumber yard gate locks, flammables cabinet, and paint mixer
   are intact.
3. Power up POS lanes, self-checkouts, and the price-check kiosks.
4. Pull and count cash drawers from the safe; record openings in the
   cash-handling log.
5. Brief the opening shift: today's promos, stock callouts, expected
   deliveries.
6. Open service desk, Pro Desk, and key cutting kiosk on schedule.

## During business hours
- Manager-on-duty checks the floor every two hours.
- Cash drops every $2,000 in a lane to the office safe.
- Department associates do hourly spot-checks of their aisles for
  fall hazards, blocked exits, and customer activity.

## Closing (30 minutes before doors close)
1. Lock the lumber yard gate (no new yard pulls after this point).
2. Power down the paint mixer; close the flammables cabinet.
3. Make last-call announcement at 10 minutes; lock front doors at close
   time but let in-store customers finish.

## Closing (after doors close)
1. Lanes count down; manager reconciles deposit.
2. Walk every aisle: lights down to dim, ladders parked, carts collected.
3. Set alarm, log out the security keypad.
4. Sweep the parking lot for customer cars / abandoned merchandise.
5. Manager-on-duty signs the close log in the office.

## Power outage / emergency close
If the store loses power for more than 15 minutes, the manager-on-duty
may declare an emergency close. POS goes offline; cash-only sales are
not permitted. Customers in-store are escorted out, doors are locked,
and district is notified within 30 minutes.
