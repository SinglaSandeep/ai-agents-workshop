# Products Knowledge Base

## Fitness Watch
The Zava Pulse Fitness Watch tracks heart rate, sleep quality, steps, and guided workouts. It has a seven-day battery life and is priced at $149.

## Smart Bottle
The Zava Hydrate Smart Bottle tracks water intake and reminds users to drink throughout the day. It syncs with the mobile app and is priced at $39.

## Home Hub
The Zava Home Hub connects wellness devices, shows daily goals, and supports voice reminders. It is priced at $99 and works best with the fitness watch and smart bottle.

## Warranty
All Zava devices include a one-year limited warranty. Extended protection is available for an additional fee at checkout.
