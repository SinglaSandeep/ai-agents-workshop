# Marketing Knowledge Base

## Spring Wellness Campaign
The Spring Wellness campaign promotes device bundles for customers starting new fitness routines. The main offer is 15 percent off a fitness watch and smart bottle bundle.

## Brand Voice
Zava uses a clear, supportive, and practical brand voice. Copy should avoid exaggerated health claims and focus on measurable habits, daily progress, and customer confidence.

## Audience Segments
Primary segments include busy professionals, caregivers, and fitness beginners. Campaigns should emphasize simple routines and low-friction setup.

## Channel Guidance
Email is best for retention offers, social is best for product education, and in-store signage should focus on bundle value and quick comparison points.
