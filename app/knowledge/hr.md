# HR Knowledge Base

## Paid Time Off
Zava Corporation provides 20 days of paid time off per calendar year for full-time employees. New employees accrue PTO after their first 30 days. Managers should approve PTO requests at least two weeks before planned leave when possible.

## Benefits
Employees are eligible for medical, dental, vision, life insurance, and a wellness stipend. Benefits enrollment opens during onboarding and during the annual enrollment window each November.

## Remote Work
Hybrid employees may work remotely up to three days per week with manager approval. Teams should keep core collaboration hours from 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM local time.

## Employee Handbook
The handbook covers conduct expectations, workplace safety, expense reimbursement, travel, and escalation paths for policy questions. Employees should contact HR for exceptions or policy interpretation.
